Last updated: July 2026

SAFEY is a student-built safety planning and resource application created by Amrik Majumdar and Atiksh Gupta.

It is not emergency services, legal advice, medical advice, mental health care, domestic violence advocacy, or a substitute for professional safety planning.

## Emergency And Crisis Situations

If someone is in immediate danger, they should contact local emergency services or a trusted local crisis resource when it is safe to do so.

## Safety Limits

Technology can fail. Browser storage, device notifications, SMS links, phone links, offline behavior, stealth mode, and external resources may behave differently depending on the device, browser, network, operating system, and user settings.

## Human Review

Trusted-contact messages, safety plans, and resource links should be reviewed carefully before use. The app should not be relied on as the only safety measure in a dangerous situation.
